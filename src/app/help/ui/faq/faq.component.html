What is Coinkeeper?

Coinkeeper is an easy-to-use platform for tracking your finances, helping you to be in charge of your own finances.

How do I use Coinkeeper?

To get started, create an account with us to access your personalized dashboard. You can view your expenses, add an expense by clicking the + icon, manually type the details or use our AI (still in beta) to fill them out, and press the add icon to add the expense.

How do I Change the theme?

You can change the theme anytime you are with use by clicking the theme icon on the top right of the screen.

How can I set budgets?

Setting a budget is a breeze. Just head to your account page and click on "change monthly budget" to set the budget value.

Can I remove a transaction?

It can be easily done through the transactions tab of the webapp. Open the tab, and you will see your past transactions. You can change the date range to see the transactions in a specific period. Click on any transaction to remove it.

Why do I need Coinkeeper?

In the ever-growing inflation era, it is very easy to not realize what and where you are spending your hard-earned money. Keeping track of your spending is extremely crucial in the current era. But most people do not know how they could do it? Traditional tools like account books, diaries, and excel may seem daunting and tedious. I agree that there are a lot of digital solutions, but they are again something which you have to learn how to use. My target while building this was to make it easy for everyone, since they should focus on earning and saving, and tracking should not be a hassle.

How secure is my data?

We never store passwords in our database, all the user data including transactions is encrypted in transit as well as in storage using AES-256 encryption.
